ABOUT ME

Who I Am

Hello! I'm Mitchel Friend, also known as TheUpDoge or AltF4U in various online communities. I'm a passionate game developer, programmer, and digital artist with a love for creating immersive experiences.

My journey in game development began with a fascination for how games could transport players to different worlds and tell compelling stories. This led me to explore various game engines and programming languages, eventually creating my own games.

I'm the developer behind Fantasy World Souls, a challenging RPG that combines classic RPG elements with souls-like difficulty. The game is available on Steam and represents my approach to game design: challenging but rewarding experiences with depth and narrative.

When I'm not coding or designing games, I enjoy exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, and connecting with other developers in the community.
